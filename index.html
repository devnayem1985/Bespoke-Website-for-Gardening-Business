Crafting Landscapes
That Inspire

At Summer Breeze Garden Design, we believe that exceptional outdoor spaces are born from the marriage of natural beauty and thoughtful design. Each project is a unique collaboration, carefully crafted to reflect your lifestyle and enhance your connection to the natural world.

Our approach combines contemporary design principles with a deep understanding of native flora, creating sustainable landscapes that evolve beautifully through the seasons while requiring minimal maintenance.

Meet Autumn
Principal Designer

With over 15 years of experience in landscape architecture and garden design, Autumn brings a unique perspective that combines formal training with an intuitive understanding of how people interact with outdoor spaces.

Her work has been featured in leading design publications and she holds a Master's degree in Landscape Architecture from the University of Melbourne. Autumn's approach is deeply rooted in sustainability and creating gardens that enhance both the natural environment and the lives of those who inhabit them.
